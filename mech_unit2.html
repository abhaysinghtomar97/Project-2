UNIT 2: Electric Vehicle and Hybride Vehicle

Electric Vehicles

Definition

Those Vehicles which fully or partially powered by Battery.

1.Battery Electric Vehicle (AEV/BEV) : They are 100% powered by battrey and no IC engine inside vehicle.

2.Hybride Electric Vehicle (HCV) : In HEV's have other source of Power transmission by Diseal engine or Petrol Engine.

3.Plug-in Hybride Electric Vehicle (PHCV) : In PHEV's have other source of Power transmission by Diseal engine or Petrol Engine along with Plug to Charge at Power Station.

Components Of Electric Vehicles

Batteries: Function of Battery to store Energy in Dc as per requirment and will flow electric charge towards Motor through Inverter.

Power Inverter : To change Dc to Ac towards Motor from regenerative Breaking system .

Controllers :To Regulate Enlectrical engery from batteries through inverter towrd motor.

  • In EV's When we press pedal more volatge flow towards motor from Battery the function of controller to control the voltage in order to optimise the performance of vehicle.

    • Electric Motor : To convert Electrical Energy into Kinetic Energy and also include regenerative breaking system .

    Charger : There are Two Types of Charger:

    1. On Board Charger(Inside car)
    2. Off Board Charger(Station)

    DC/DC : to Convert High voltage direct current into low voltage direct current , required for vehicle Accessories.

    Thermal Cooling System : To maintain proper temprature for battrey , motor and other components.

    Motivation Towards Electric Vehicles

    1. Provide Energy Security
    2. Reduction Fuel dependency
    3. Reduction in Maintenance Cost
    4. provide fuel diversification
    5. Reduction in Green House Gases

    Benifits of Electric Vehicles

    1. Reduction in Running Cost
    2. Reduction in Maintenance Cost
    3. Tax and Financial Benifits
    4. Reduction in Pollution
    5. Easy to Drive and Quiet
    6. Reduction in Green House Gases

    Disadvantage of Electric Vehicles

    1. High initial Cost of Vehicle
    2. Less Range
    3. Charging Time
    4. Less Charging Station
    5. Recycling of Batteries
